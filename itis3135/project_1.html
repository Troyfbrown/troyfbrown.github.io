Project 1

Musical Education by Aubrie Lynn

Project Overview

  • This application will be a website intended to advertise Aubrie Lynn as a marching arts percussion instructor.
  • Intended users of this website will be band or percussion directors looking to hire staff. As well as potential students looking for Private Lessons
  • The Content Will Include a home page introfucing the site, an about page describing who Aubrie is, a page to describe her services and rates, a few pages detailing her experience in different subjects, and a page listing her availability.
